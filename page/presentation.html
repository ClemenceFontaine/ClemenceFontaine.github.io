Based between Brussels and Nancy, I am interested in multidisciplinary practices and recently thinking about the notion of meteorology as an example of a relationship between a society and its environment. I'm very interested by the Environnemental Humanities and his importance in our age.
First graduated in Communication at ENSAD in Nancy, I then continued my studies at Erg in Brussels in 'Design et Politique du Multiple'. There I develop a practice halfway between video installation and Graphic Design.
