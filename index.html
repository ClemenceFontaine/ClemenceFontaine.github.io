Nomad research; I am interested in multidisciplinary practices and recently, thinking about the notion of meteorology as an example of a relationship between a society and its environment. How can we co-construct our world ? I am very captivated by the Environmental Humanities and their importance in our time.

First graduated in Communication at ENSAD Nancy, I continued my studies at Erg in Brussels in master 'Design et Politique du Multiple'. There I develop a practice halfway between research, graphism, documentaries and art.
