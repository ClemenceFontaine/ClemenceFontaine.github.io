Clémence Fontaine



Nomadic designer; interested in to multidisciplinary practices and recently started a research on the concept of meteorology as an example of a relationship between a society and its environment. How can we co-construct our world ? Very captivated by the Environmental Humanities and their importance in our time.

Graduate in communication at ENSAD Nancy, I continued my studies at the Erg in Brussels in master "Design et Politique du Multiple". I have developed a practice halfway between research, graphic design, documentary and art.
